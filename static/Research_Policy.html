Welcome to Hamadan Craft Revival Foundation

An ecosystem where art and artisans flourish

We are undertaking a pioneering initiative to develop a comprehensive policy framework that enables artisans, safeguards traditional techniques, and forges a path forward in the global market through rigorous scientific inquiry.

The Foundation works as a think tank, engaging in research, policy analysis, and advocacy to protect the rich heritage of Kashmiri crafts while addressing the modern challenges faced by artisans.

Explore Our Initiatives | Get Involved

What HCRF Does?

Our mission is to offer practical, evidence-based solutions and alternatives that not only address the current challenges in the Kashmir Handicraft Industry but also capitalize on future opportunities, ensuring long-term growth and sustainability.

Research and Analysis

We provide in-depth research & analysis on specific policy issues on Kashmir Craft Industry, offering evidence-based solutions & alternatives

Policy
Development

Think tanks help in crafting effective policies for Kashmir Handicraft Sector by drawing on expertise from various fields and perspectives

Public
Discourse

We stimulate public discourse on Kashmir Craft by hosting discussions, publishing research findings, and engaging with the media.

Government Advising

Think tank serve as advisors to governments of India and Jammu & Kashmir, providing guidance on policy decisions and strategies

CraftLore

Science backed Open Virtual University of Kashmir Craft's - gateway to Kashmir Craft knowledge Center, from the intricate techniques employed to ensuring authenticity and navigating the complexities of counterfeits. Developed with ForceSource, San Francisco based global leader in Repository Documentation using Big & Meta Data.

ARTSTAY

ArtStay goes beyond a typical travel platform, innovative portal connecting Kashmiri artisans with global art travelers. It's a movement fostering a unique exchange, offering two captivating programs, Kashmir Craft Safaris and Vacation with Kashmiri Artisans. Global Tourism Forum brings its extensive network and industry knowledge to the table and impact

Offshore Integration

Omnichannel strategy integrating US based physical brick-and-mortar stores & wearhouse with robust free e-commerce platform for Kashmir based struggling entrepreneurs. This also includes Re-commerce, Import and Facilitation. These interventions are supported by De Koshur Craft USA, plugin the gap of Trust and Credibility, & Improved Customer Service
