Partner Network

Who We Collaborate With

HCRF Partner Network

HCRF’s global Partner Network includes leaders, innovators, and institutions committed to advancing Kashmir’s handicrafts, bringing expertise to elevate heritage and empower artisans

International Trade Organizations

We collaborate with trade bodies to expand market access, ensuring Kashmiri artisans reach global premium markets and buyers.

Cultural Preservation Institutions

Leading museums and institutions partner with HCRF to preserve Kashmir’s craft traditions through research, exhibitions, and global education

Technology & Innovation Leaders

Blockchain, traceability, and e-commerce drive the future of Kashmiri crafts. Tech partnerships empower artisans with tools for authentication and global sales access.

Ethical Trade Organizations

Sustainability advocates and ethical trade networks help Kashmiri artisans adopt eco-friendly methods, reduce carbon footprint, and ensure fair wages and safe conditions

Academic Institutions

Partnerships with universities and research centers produce scholarly research, informing best practices, policy, and innovation, enhancing Kashmir’s cultural and economic understanding.

NGOs and Non-Profits

Aligned with NGOs and non-profits, we empower artisans through training, capacity building, and welfare support, ensuring sustainable handicraft growth.
