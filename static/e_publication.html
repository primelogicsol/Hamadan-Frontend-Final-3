Our publications focus on preserving the rich heritage of traditional crafts while driving forward sustainable, modern practices. Explore a curated collection of studies, reports, and case analyses that shape the future of the handicraft industry.

Empowering Through Knowledge, Innovation & Preservation

What HCRF Does?

At the Hamadan Craft Revival Foundation e Publications, we curate an exceptional body of knowledge dedicated to the preservation, elevation, and scholarly exploration of Kashmiri crafts. Our offerings encompass a diverse array of meticulously researched content designed to inform, inspire, and empower artisans, academics, policymakers, and industry professionals alike.

Best Practices

Delve into rigorously developed guides that integrate sustainable methods, ethical practices, and innovative techniques with the timeless traditions of Kashmiri craftsmanship. These resources serve as essential frameworks for artisans and stakeholders aiming to balance heritage with modernity.

www.craftlore.dekoshurcrafts.com

Case Studies

Our collection of case studies presents detailed, real-world analyses of the challenges and triumphs faced within the global and local handicraft sectors. These narratives provide invaluable lessons in resilience, adaptability, and innovation, offering blueprints for the continued success of traditional crafts in contemporary markets.

www.artstay.dekoshurcrafts.com

Research Papers

We offer access to comprehensive academic studies that examine the cultural, economic, and environmental dimensions of Kashmiri crafts. These scholarly papers contribute to the critical discourse on preserving cultural heritage while adapting to evolving global contexts.

www.dekoshurcrafts.com

Driving the Future of Kashmiri Craftsmanship

Membership-Based Access


Driving the Future of Kashmiri Craftsmanship By choosing membership access, you’re not just gaining exclusive insights; you are becoming a key force behind the creation of high-impact, research-driven publications that safeguard and propel Kashmiri craftsmanship into the future. Your support is instrumental in expanding our knowledge base, advancing cutting-edge research, and equipping artisans, scholars, and industry leaders with the vital tools and resources they need to excel. Your membership fuels the growth of publications that not only preserve the legacy of Kashmiri crafts but also ensure their global prominence and long-term sustainability. Join us in this transformative mission to elevate, innovate, and empower the craft sector—your involvement will shape the future of this cultural heritage.

Artisan Membership

Empowers artisans with tools, insights, and training for global exposure and business growth. Gain direct access to craft industry trends and exclusive mentorship to elevate your craft to international standards. Artisans will also benefit from business development strategies designed to enhance market reach and profitability.

Individual Membership

Craft enthusiasts receive full access to publications, events, and networking opportunities. Stay connected with the heart of Kashmiri craftsmanship and explore authentic traditions through curated content. As an individual member, you also get early invitations to exclusive craft exhibitions and cultural events.

Professional Membership

Ideal for industry experts and scholars, offering exclusive reports, collaborations, and market analysis. Access cutting-edge research and stay ahead with the latest trends and innovations in the craft sector. Professionals will have opportunities to collaborate on research, joint ventures, and global craft initiatives.

Corporate Membership

Supports companies with business development resources, artisan collaborations, and CSR opportunities. Position your business as a leader in sustainable craftsmanship while fostering partnerships with local artisans. Corporate members can showcase their brand’s commitment to cultural preservation through customized CSR initiatives.

Patron Membership

Patrons gain recognition and exclusive access to VIP events, consultations, and lifelong publication archives. Your support plays a pivotal role in preserving cultural heritage and empowering the Kashmiri artisan community. As a patron, you will also receive personal invitations to high-level discussions and cultural forums on global craft preservation.

Student Membership

Provides students discounted access to research, internships, and networking with industry professionals. Get firsthand exposure to the world of Kashmiri crafts while building valuable connections for your future career. Students can also access specialized workshops and internships that deepen their understanding of traditional crafts.
