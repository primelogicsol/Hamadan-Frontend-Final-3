Share Your Research With Global Audience

HCRF welcomes researchers dedicated to Kashmiri crafts, artisan livelihoods, and sustainability to publish their work on our e-Publications platform. By sharing your research, you’ll be part of a growing knowledge base that informs the craft industry and provides valuable support to artisans. Your contributions will help raise global awareness and understanding of Kashmir’s rich craft heritage.
