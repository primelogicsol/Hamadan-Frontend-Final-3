Industry Research

Building Knowledge for a Sustainable Craft Future

Empowering Artisans through In-Depth Industry Research

The Kashmir handicraft industry is a testament to the region's rich cultural heritage, spanning centuries of intricate craftsmanship. With its vibrant history, each craft carries the legacy of generations of artisans who have mastered the art. In today’s globalized economy, researching this unique industry offers valuable insights into its evolving landscape, challenges faced by artisans, and the need for modernization without compromising authenticity.

This research initiative is aimed at fostering a sustainable future while preserving the deep-rooted traditions that define Kashmir's art and craft.

Kashmir Handicraft Industry Research

Research Objectives: Defining Paths to Craft Sustainability and Growth

Preserving culture, driving innovation, promoting artisan welfare.

HCRF’s research seeks to preserve Kashmir’s crafts by promoting sustainable practices, improving market access, and advocating for gender equity. The research will also integrate blockchain for authenticity and enhance policy frameworks to protect artisans. These objectives will ensure the long-term sustainability and global recognition of Kashmiri crafts.
