Membership Opportunities

We offer various membership categories to individuals and organizations that wish to support our mission and engage with the thriving world of Kashmiri handicrafts.

Artisan Membership

Support artisans directly, access business resources, join policy discussions, and participate in programs that enhance livelihoods and craft development.

Individual Membership

Contribute to advocacy and research efforts, stay updated on industry developments, and participate in exclusive events to support Kashmir crafts.

Professional Membership

Ideal for craft-related professionals, offering opportunities to collaborate on projects, gain insights, and influence the craft industry’s future.

Corporate Membership

Corporations supporting artisans can benefit from CSR opportunities, contribute to preservation, and gain visibility as patrons of Kashmir crafts.

Patron Membership

Significant contributors help fund projects, influence policies, and preserve Kashmiri crafts, playing a pivotal role in heritage conservation.

Student Membership

For students passionate about cultural heritage, offering research, internship opportunities, and participation in meaningful projects for craft advocacy.

Preserve Tradition, Empower Artisans, Shape the Future

Why Join HCRF?

Becoming a member of the Hamdan Craft Revival Foundation offers exclusive opportunities to contribute to the preservation and advancement of Kashmiri handicrafts.

Exclusive Access

As a member, you’ll gain early access to cutting-edge research publications, detailed industry reports, and insights into emerging policy trends. Stay ahead of developments that affect the handicraft sector and understand how shifts in policies and markets will impact artisans. You can better advocate for sustainable practices and participate in shaping solutions that preserve the rich heritage of Kashmiri crafts for future generations.

Networking

Join a vibrant community of artisans, industry leaders, and policymakers dedicated to preserving Kashmir’s craftsmanship. HCRF membership offers opportunities to connect with influential figures passionate about safeguarding traditional crafts and building a sustainable future for artisans. Build relationships, exchange knowledge, and collaborate on initiatives.

Impact

Your membership directly supports the protection and promotion of Kashmir’s cultural heritage. Through advocacy, help influence policies, improve artisans' working conditions, and create sustainable opportunities. Together, we work to safeguard endangered craft techniques, provide artisans with tools, and ensure their craft is respected and recognized. Your involvement helps shape a brighter future for Kashmiri crafts.

Events & Workshops

HCRF members enjoy access to exclusive events and workshops addressing the key challenges in the handicraft sector. These events unite experts, artisans, and leaders to discuss topics like sustainability, innovation, and market trends. Participate in hands-on workshops, gain new skills, explore innovative solutions, and engage in discussions that shape the future of Kashmir’s handicraft industry.
