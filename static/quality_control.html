HCRF Handicrafts Business Networking


The HCRF Handicrafts Business Networking platform is an unparalleled powerhouse designed to forge strategic global connections for artisans, entrepreneurs, and businesses. This meticulously curated infrastructure is your gateway to accessing elite international markets, unlocking high-value partnerships, and establishing a global presence. Whether you are targeting discerning international buyers, showcasing your craft at world-renowned trade events, or seeking transformative business alliances, our platform positions you at the forefront of the global handicraft industry.

In this portal, you’re not just a reader—you are part of a movement to preserve and celebrate the world of Kashmiri crafts. And with each page you turn, you support the artisans who dedicate their lives to keeping these centuries-old traditions alive. Begin your journey today, and let the magic of Kashmiri craftsmanship inspire you.

By harnessing the power of our extensive global database of industry leaders, you gain exclusive access to premier business listings, elite trade partners, and a wealth of buyer and supplier intelligence. Our platform elevates your business by connecting you with the highest levels of industry decision-makers across continents.

Mission, Focus, and Reach

Protecting Kashmir Crafts & Empowering Artisan Communities

Our Mission

  • Revitalize crafts through preservation & innovation.
  • Advance Kashmir's craftsmanship with research.
  • Promoting eco-friendly, sustainable handicrafts.
  • Support policies protecting Kashmiri artisans.
  • Forge impactful partnerships for our initiatives.
  • Empowering artisans with resources & tools.

Core Focus Areas

  • Market research and analysis.
  • Policy research and advocacy.
  • Skill development and capacity building.
  • Cultural preservation and documentation.
  • Market access facilitation.
  • Innovation in product design and development.

Our Audience

  • Artisans and craft communities.
  • Government agencies and policymakers.
  • NGOs and development organizations.
  • Private sector stakeholders.
  • Educational institutions & training centers.
  • General public interested in art and crafts.

CraftLore

Science backed Open Virtual University of Kashmir Craft's - gateway to Kashmir Craft knowledge Center, from the intricate techniques employed to ensuring authenticity and navigating the complexities of counterfeits. Developed with ForceSource, San Francisco based global leader in Repository Documentation using Big & Meta Data.

www.craftlore.dekoshurcrafts.com

ARTSTAY

ArtStay goes beyond a typical travel platform, innovative portal connecting Kashmiri artisans with global art travelers. It's a movement fostering a unique exchange, offering two captivating programs, Kashmir Craft Safaris and Vacation with Kashmiri Artisans. Global Tourism Forum brings its extensive network and industry knowledge to the table, elevating our reach and impact

www.artstay.dekoshurcrafts.com

Offshore Integration

Omnichannel strategy integrating US based physical brick-and-mortar stores & wearhouse with robust free e-commerce platform for Kashmir based struggling entrepreneurs. This also includes Re-commerce, Import and Exhibition Facilitation. These interventions are supported by De Koshur Craft USA, plugin the gap of Trust and Credibility, & Improved Customer Service

www.dekoshurcrafts.com
